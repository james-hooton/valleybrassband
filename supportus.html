Support Us

In order to support the band and its players, and maintain the necessary equipment and facilities. we engage in various fundraising activities throughout the year, including caroling and collections during the Christmas season. Our events page provides information on our upcoming performances and collections.

Additionally, we have partnered with Give as you Live Online and Amazon Smile to provide supporters with easy and convenient ways to donate to the band while they shop online. Both services are free to use, and Give as you Live Online offers the option to make one-time or recurring donations. Please visit the links below for more information and to sign up.
