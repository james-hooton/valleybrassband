Welcome to Valley Brass Band

We are a brass band based in Haydock, St. Helens, in the North West of England. The band was founded in 1992 when Musical Director, David Chadwick, came to the realisation that there were not enough musical services for young children in the area. We are now an established group and performing in concerts and contests regionally, nationally, and occasionally internationally.

We run a learner group, training band and adult training band to ensure anyone can have access to learning a musical instrument, whether you are entirely new, or have not played in a long time.
